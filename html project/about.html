CLUB FOR APPLIED SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY

Introduction

The world of science and its various branches is an ever-growing and now certainly evergreen field of work and an integral part of economical growth in modern societies. Understanding and learning STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Math) requires rigourous theoretical understanding of subject matters and apt, precise implementation of it in real world projects. Scientists and mathematicians are consistent problem solvers and boast strong logical as well as creative minds.
The Club for Applied Science and Technology (CAST) aims to bring science out of just homes, it aims to bring problem solving and logical implementation outside of just exams and it aims to bring out creativity and logical knowledge of individuals into the real world practices and olympiads. The various events that CAST aims to organize in the upcoming academic sessions will allow students to not only excel their minds in STEM but also to form groups and build firm team-spirit and quality of leadership amongst themselves.

Events

  • Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics and Biology Olympiads.
  • Hackathons (Computer Science Olympiad and software design contest)
  • Talk show with industry experts in STEM
  • Collaboration with industry experts such as Karkhana for conducting bootcamps in Robotics, AI, Coding etc.
  • Publish a journal of events conducted at the end of the session.
